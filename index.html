tips

  • brainstorm

    When writing a poem you need a theme; an idea. You need a beginning, middle,and end to your anything that involves writing. So brainstorming is the best tactic. This helps you write your ideas down and make it easier for you to write. It gives you an idea of what the purpose and message of the poem is!

  • learn from other poets!

    Looking at other poems and seeing what other poets have wrote helps you do better. It gives you a gist and idea of what is expected in yours as well. Though, you should not use this for the content part of your poem. This is another tip to help you to write poems! There are many other more to make you write the perfect one!
